## [Digging into CTM's Consciousness: A Possible Mechanism for CTM Generating Self-Conscious](/talks/ctm/)

**Shaoyang Cui**, Shanglin Wu, Nikolai Madlener
<br>*IJTCS 2023 Workshop* | Supervised by Prof. Lenore Blum (CMU) and Prof. Manuel Blum (CMU)

Based on the Conscious Turing Machine (CTM) framework, we proposed a clear definition of self-consciousness in CTM and designed the **MIT model** --- three specialized processors (Model-of-the-World, Instruction-Generator, Thought-Generator) --- as a possible mechanism for generating self-aware processing. We validated the model using visual illusions and phantom limb phenomena.
