## FAB: Factory of Abstract-style Benchmark
*Independent Project* (Nov 2024 -- May 2025)

- Developed the first fully automated, low-cost benchmark generation framework for abstract-style evaluation across multiple academic fields
- Enabled scalable benchmarking of large language models using structured abstraction errors, covering semantic, structural, and factual variants
- Open-source on GitHub: [FAB Benchmark Repository](https://github.com/spidermonk7)

## Possible Models of Self-Awareness in Conscious Turing Machines
*Course Project, supervised by Prof. Lenore Blum (CMU) and Prof. Manuel Blum (CMU)* (Sep 2022 -- Dec 2022)

- Discussed consciousness and self-consciousness of Conscious Turing Machines (CTM) and proposed a possible model and workflow (the 'MIT' model)
- Invited to present at the IJTCS 2023 workshop
